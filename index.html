Sotiria's Radio Domain

The academic year 2025-2026 completes my first year at Xoros 94.2 radio station. As I move on to my second year, I have plenty of ideas for shows. So, the main "theme" of my show is basically chit-chat. Local talk, music, tech news, or whatever else sparks my interest that day. Also, if you have any recommendations, of course I am open to them (you should go to the "contact" section). Every Saturday at 4, I'll keep you company at xoros.samos.aegean.gr (or FM 94.2), starting Saturday the 11th of October. See ya.
